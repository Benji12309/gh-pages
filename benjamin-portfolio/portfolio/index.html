About Me

Hey, I’m Benjamin, and I’m currently studying at Saint Paul College in the USA, working towards my Associate of Science in Computer Science. I’m planning to graduate in Summer 2025. Growing up in Africa, I’ve always been obsessed with technology. As a kid, I’d spend hours exploring how tech works, and it was so fascinating to me how addictive and cool it could be. Over time, that curiosity turned into a real passion, and I started learning about the endless possibilities that technology offers.

Outside of school, I’m big into gaming — it’s something I’ve loved for years, and I’m always keeping up with the latest gaming trends. I also enjoy shopping and fun things that catch my eye. I’m always looking for ways to improve my skills, and I’m excited to keep diving deeper into the tech world, whether that’s through coding, game development, or exploring new tech innovations. I’m super excited for the future and can’t wait to see what I’ll learn next!

I have taken the following classes at Saint Paul College:

  • Web Fundamentals, where I learned HTML and CSS
  • Computer Science and Information Systems
  • Introduction to Computing and Programming Concepts
